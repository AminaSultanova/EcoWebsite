Our Projects

Project 1: EcoTracker App

EcoTracker is a mobile app that helps people measure their daily carbon footprint. It tracks energy use, transportation, and water consumption, and provides tips on how to reduce environmental impact. Thousands of users already rely on it to make greener choices.

Project 2: SmartWaste Platform

SmartWaste is a digital solution for smarter waste management in cities. It helps municipalties optimize garbage collection routes, monitor recycling levels, and encourage citizens to sort waste properly. As a result, cities can reduce CO2 emissions and improve sustainability.

Project 3: SolarHome Kit

SolarHome Kit is an affordable solar energy solution for households. It provides easy installation of solar panels, real-time energy monitoring via a mobile app, and helps families save money while switching to renewable power sources.
